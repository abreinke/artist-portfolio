Storm Symphony

This project is meant to create thunderstorm sounds, some of which were original recordings. It's an extremely personal piece since I had a phobia of thunderstorms for awhile. I eventually got past this fear, but I want to help other people overcome similar weather-related phobias.

    Directions for Generating the Thunderstorm

  • To start a downpour, press the R key.
  • To create a thunder crack, press the T key.
  • To create hail sounds, press the H key.
  • To create wind, press the W key.
  • To increase the volume, press the A key.
  • To decrease the volume, press the D key.
  • To stop the storm, press the P key.
